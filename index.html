Hi, I'm Amelita

I've recently started a pyrography - or woodburning - business, called Bespoke Pyro Co. My custom designs will soon be found on Etsy.

I offer three types of woodburnt gifts: pun coasters, puzzle coasters, and personalized puzzles.
Pun coasters have minimalistic pictures of a coast line, along with the name of the coast, such as "east" or "west". Taken all together, you have East Coasters for East Coasters, West Coasters for West Coasters, etc! They can be personalized with a dot, heart or x on the exact locale of the recipient.
Puzzle coasters are coaster sets of 6 which combined display a picture of your choosing. Puzzles are the same except - puzzles! All pieces are hand made and finished with polyurethane.
